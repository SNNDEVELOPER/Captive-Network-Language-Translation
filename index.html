Introduction

  1. Unable to translate your website into other languages due to restrictions on a captive network? This example was created as a workaround for just such an occasion.

Instructions

  1. Include language.js in the footer of your page(s). <script src="language.js"></script>
  2. Add <div id="langSelect"></div> on your page and style your way.
  3. Add data-translate="somevalue" to any and all html elements you wish to have translated like <div data-translate="welcome"></div>.
  4. Add additional entries to the language.js portalDict object for your appropriate languages.
  5. Add additional language selections to the portalLang object in language.js.
  6. Modify default language by editing defaultLanguage = "EN" in language.js.
  7. Handy script for pages and sites that are on a restrictive network or walled garden and lack external translation API access.
